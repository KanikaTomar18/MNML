Process

How Your Order Travels

1
Order Confirmed

You'll receive a confirmation email within minutes of placing your order. We review every order by hand before dispatching.

2
Packed with Care

Your items are wrapped in acid-free tissue, nestled in a kraft box, and sealed. Fragile items receive additional padding.

3
Dispatched

Orders placed before 2PM on weekdays ship the same day. You'll receive a tracking number via SMS and email.

4
Delivered

Our courier partners will attempt delivery twice. If missed, the package is held at a local facility for 5 days.

Good to Know

Packaging & Sustainability

♻️
100% Recycled Outer Box

Our shipping boxes are made from post-consumer recycled cardboard. No plastic tape — we use paper tape with a water-activated adhesive.

🌿
No Plastic Filler

Fragile items are cushioned with shredded recycled paper or honeycomb kraft wrap — never bubble wrap or styrofoam pellets.

📦
Right-Size Boxes

We use the smallest box that safely fits your order. Fewer emissions per shipment, less waste in your bin.
