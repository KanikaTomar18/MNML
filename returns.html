Step by Step

How to Return

Email us within 30 days

Write to orders@mnml.in with your order number and which item(s) you're returning. No need to explain why.

We book the pickup

Our team schedules a courier pickup from your address within 2 business days, at no cost to you.

Pack it as it came

Place the item in its original packaging (or similar). The courier will collect at your door.

Refund within 7 days

Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund is processed within 5–7 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email.

Policy Details

What Can Be Returned

✓ Returnable Items
  • All ceramics and homeware
  • Unlit candles in original packaging

✗ Non-Returnable
  • Candles that have been lit
  • Opened skincare & personal care

  • Items damaged by misuse
